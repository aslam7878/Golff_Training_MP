Our Expertise

With strong technical expertise and practical experience, GOLFF SARL provides high-impact consulting services across Human Resources, Tax, Legal, and Management. Our areas of expertise include:

  • Human Resources Strategy Development

    We partner with you to design, communicate, and implement a human resources strategy aligned with your organization’s goals.

  • Strategic Workforce Planning

    We guide your organization in conducting targeted workforce planning to meet current and future needs.

  • Talent Acquisition

    We help your organization attract top talent through customized recruitment strategies.

  • Onboarding Processes

    We design onboarding processes that integrate new employees smoothly and efficiently.

  • Performance Management

    We develop performance management systems that drive accountability and improvement.

  • Compensation and Benefits

    We assess and optimize your compensation and benefits systems.

  • HR Policy Development

    We craft and refine HR policies that streamline operations and ensure compliance.

  • Learning and Development

    We create development programs that enhance workforce skills and agility.

  • Organizational Development and Change Management

    We support your organization through change with structured development plans.

  • Employee Engagement

    We facilitate engagement surveys and develop action plans to boost motivation.

  • Employee Relations and Compliance

    We guide organizations in maintaining positive employee relations while ensuring compliance.

  • Incident Management and Conflict Resolution

    We provide expert support in managing safeguarding, safety, and compliance incidents.

  • HR Audits and Systems

    We conduct HR audits and optimize HR systems like payroll.

  • Human Resources Information Systems (HRIS)

    We implement HRIS systems for efficient data management and reporting.

  • Offboarding Processes

    We manage offboarding with compliance and professionalism.
